Anime

Chainsaw Man

Chainsaw Man (Japanese: チェンソーマン
Hepburn: Chensō Man) is a Japanese
manga series written and illustrated by
Tatsuki Fujimoto. Its first part was serialized
in Shueisha's shōnen manga magazine Weekly Shōnen Jump from December 2018 to December 2020; its second part began serialization in Shueisha's Shōnen Jump+ online magazine in July 2022. Its chapters have been collected in 12 tankōbon volumes as of October 2022.
